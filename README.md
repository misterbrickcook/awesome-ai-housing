## Tenant Communication

- [EliseAI](https://eliseai.com/) — AI-powered platform automating tenant communication across phone, email, SMS, and chat for multifamily properties.
- [AppFolio Realm-X](https://www.appfolio.com/ai) — Generative AI assistant embedded in the AppFolio property management platform, automating leasing responses and maintenance triage.
- [Voiceflow for Property Management](https://www.voiceflow.com/ai/property-management) — No-code platform for building custom AI chatbots and voice assistants to handle tenant inquiries.
- [TailorTalk](https://tailortalk.ai/) — AI chatbot providing 24/7 tenant support, automating responses to common questions and routing complex issues to staff.
- [DoorLoop](https://www.doorloop.com/) — Property management software with AI chatbot integrations for automated tenant communication workflows.

## Predictive Maintenance

- [BrainBox AI](https://brainboxai.com/en/) — Autonomous AI that predicts building conditions and self-adjusts HVAC systems every 5 minutes, reducing energy consumption by up to 25%.
- [T2D2](https://t2d2.ai) — AI-driven building inspection platform using computer vision to detect facade and structural deterioration from drone imagery.
- [Facilio](https://facilio.com/) — AI-powered property operations platform aggregating IoT sensor data to enable predictive maintenance and automated work order management.
- [Verv Energy](https://verv.energy/) — High-frequency energy monitoring with machine learning to predict HVAC equipment failures and optimize building performance.
- [Cohesion Savvy](https://www.cohesionib.com/products/savvy) — Predictive AI solution centralizing building system data to anticipate equipment issues and optimize operations.

## Energy & ESG

- [Deepki](https://www.deepki.com/) — ESG data intelligence SaaS used by 600+ real estate companies across 38 countries for sustainability tracking and decarbonization planning.
- [BuildingMinds](https://buildingminds.com/) — Schindler-backed AI platform for real estate decarbonization, automating ESG reporting (GRESB, SFDR, EU Taxonomy) and stranded asset risk prediction.
- [Cambio](https://cambio.ai/) — AI-native CRE operations platform using LLMs to automate building data collection and identify energy cost savings across portfolios.
- [Siemens Building X](https://www.siemens.com/us/en/products/buildingtechnologies/building-x.html) — Cloud-based smart building suite tracking energy consumption, CO2 footprint, and building operations in real time.
- [Next Sense](https://www.next-sense.com/) — Combines AI agents and structured building data to continuously improve energy performance and reduce carbon emissions.

## Document Processing

- [Leverton (MRI Contract Intelligence)](https://leverton.ai/) — AI-powered lease abstraction using deep learning and OCR to extract data from lease documents in up to 25 languages.
- [Prophia](https://www.prophia.com/) — AI lease abstraction and insights platform for commercial real estate, having processed 140,000+ documents.
- [Propaya](https://www.propaya.com/) — AI tool that instantly abstracts commercial leases, extracting 188+ key terms with clause-cited insights.
- [Ocrolus](https://www.ocrolus.com/) — AI document automation for mortgage lenders, classifying and extracting data from 1,600+ financial document types.
- [V7 Labs](https://www.v7labs.com/blog/ai-real-estate-lease-abstraction) — AI training data and automation platform with applications for real estate lease abstraction using computer vision and NLP.

## Property Valuation

- [Zillow Zestimate](https://www.zillow.com/zestimate/) — Neural network-based AVM providing instant property value estimates for 100M+ US homes with a 1.83% on-market median error rate.
- [HouseCanary](https://www.housecanary.com/) — ML-powered AVM platform providing valuations and insights on 114M+ properties, used by major Wall Street investment banks.
- [PriceHubble](https://www.pricehubble.com/) — Swiss AI property valuation platform serving 800+ companies across 11 countries with explainable AVMs and market forecasts.
- [BatchData AVM](https://batchdata.io/avm) — API-first automated valuation model combining ML with 700+ data points per property for 128M+ properties.
- [CASAFARI](https://www.casafari.com/) — Europe's largest real estate data platform aggregating listings from 30,000+ sources across 20+ countries with ML-based property matching.
- [Matrixian AVM](https://matrixian.com/en/producten/automated-valuation-model/) — European AVM provider offering ML-based property valuations with confidence intervals for banks and investors.

## Smart Building / IoT

- [Cohesion](https://www.cohesionib.com/) — Smart building platform integrating IoT sensors, AI analytics, and access control into a unified system for real-time building management.
- [PropTechOS](https://proptechos.com/) — Open smart building platform providing a unified API layer to connect building systems, enabling AI-driven analytics across portfolios.
- [Honeywell Connected Buildings](https://www.honeywell.com/us/en/press/2025/06/honeywell-unveils-ai-powered-building-management-solution) — AI-powered building management integrating remote monitoring, predictive maintenance, and energy tracking.
- [Autodesk Forma](https://www.autodesk.com/products/forma) — AI-powered generative design platform optimizing site planning by analyzing terrain, wind, sunlight, noise, and zoning data.
- [Willow Digital Twins](https://www.willowinc.com/) — Digital twin platform for real estate portfolios using AI to optimize building operations, occupancy planning, and sustainability performance.

## Cost Accounting

- [Aareon](https://www.aareon.com/) — Europe's leading property management ERP with integrated AI for document analysis, repair diagnostics, invoice processing, and message routing.
- [hellohousing](https://hellohousing.de/) — German platform for operating cost allocations (Betriebskostenabrechnung) with AI-powered receipt capture and automated bank integration.
- [immocloud](https://www.immocloud.de/nebenkostenabrechnung/) — Property management platform automating Nebenkostenabrechnung with calculation, distribution keys, and tenant letter generation.
- [objego](https://objego.de/) — German proptech enabling private landlords to create utility cost allocations, manage expenses digitally, and prepare tax returns.
- [Doozer](https://www.doozer.de/) — SaaS platform digitizing residential renovation projects, reducing apartment refurbishment planning from weeks to under 60 minutes.

## Tenant Screening & Fraud Detection

- [Naborly](https://naborly.com/) — AI-powered tenant screening platform using machine learning to assess rental applications with risk scoring and fraud detection.
- [Snappt](https://www.snappt.com/) — AI document fraud detection for rental applications, analyzing bank statements and pay stubs to identify fabricated documents.
- [RentPrep](https://www.rentprep.com/) — Automated tenant background screening combining credit, criminal, and eviction data with ML-based risk assessment.

## Research Papers

- [Incorporating Large Language Models in Automated Real Estate Valuation](https://www.tandfonline.com/doi/full/10.1080/08965803.2025.2587313) (2025) — Explores how LLM-extracted features from property ads improve automated valuation models, tested on 9,842 transactions in Oslo.
- [On the Performance of LLMs for Real Estate Appraisal](https://arxiv.org/abs/2506.11812) (2025) — Evaluates how leading LLMs generate competitive house price estimates through optimized in-context learning strategies.
- [AI-Based on Machine Learning Methods for Urban Real Estate Prediction: A Systematic Survey](https://link.springer.com/article/10.1007/s11831-023-10010-5) (2023) — Comprehensive survey covering ML methods for urban real estate price prediction.
- [Comparative Analysis of Advanced Models for Predicting Housing Prices](https://www.mdpi.com/2413-8851/9/2/32) (2025) — Review comparing ML models for housing price prediction, finding XGBoost, SVR, and neural networks outperform traditional approaches.
- [Artificial Intelligence for Energy Optimization in Smart Buildings](https://link.springer.com/article/10.1186/s42162-025-00592-8) (2025) — Meta-analysis of 126 peer-reviewed studies on AI-driven energy optimization in buildings.
- [HELIOS: LLM for Enhanced Homogeneity Identification in Real Estate Market Analysis](https://www.mdpi.com/2076-3417/14/14/6135) (2024) — Introduces the HELIOS concept for LLM-enhanced real estate market segmentation.
- [EXPRESS: An LLM-Generated Explainable Property Valuation System](https://arxiv.org/html/2503.12344v1) (2025) — Proposes an explainable valuation system using LLMs to generate feature-wise explanations by comparing properties.
