# Contributing to Awesome AI in Housing

Thanks for your interest in contributing! This list aims to be a high-quality, curated collection — not an exhaustive directory.

## How to contribute

1. **Fork** this repository
2. **Add your link** in the appropriate category
3. **Submit a pull request** with a brief explanation of why the resource belongs here

You can also [open an issue](../../issues) if you want to suggest a resource without submitting a PR.

## What gets accepted

- Tools, platforms, or companies actively using AI in the housing or real estate industry
- Peer-reviewed research papers or preprints from reputable sources
- Open-source projects related to AI in real estate

## Format

Each entry should follow this format:

```
- [Name](https://example.com/) — One sentence describing what it does.
```

- Keep descriptions concise (one sentence)
- Use an em dash ` — ` to separate the link from the description
- Place the entry alphabetically within its category

## What doesn't belong

- Generic real estate tools without a meaningful AI component
- Paywalled content without a publicly accessible summary
- Self-promotional submissions without clear value to the community
- Broken or unreachable links

## Quality over quantity

A smaller, well-curated list is more useful than a long one. If something is borderline, it probably doesn't belong yet.
